Degree Show Posters

The design is of a folded poster, which hangs off the wall. The details are on the folded element so are easily visible to the audience, but to see the poster design involves interaction from the audience by physically unfolding the poster.
Each poster is unique, with a different angle on which it is folded. The unveiling of the poster design is not only the act which is stated on the poster but is also the act which all students partake on graduation, and the start of their profession.
The colour is overprinted with the black type, which brings a sense of depth to the type and the over printing, the CMY series and the Em and En dashes all portray themes within Graphic Design.
