WasteLess App Design

I chose to create an app to help reduce food waste and began by looking at the largest contributors to this problem, such as packaging. I started my research by obtaining informative data from users with questionnaires to enhance the UX. I then looked at the possible competition to my app, with all of this information I then created an UI.

My research included books and articles so I could explore the prevalence of this problem and in what areas. The research disclosed ways in which food waste could be reduced, including; packaging, information on best before and use by dates and ways to use food 'waste'. I used all of this evidence to design my app, in order to reduce these problems.

I am currently in the process of creating a working protype for this app concept.
