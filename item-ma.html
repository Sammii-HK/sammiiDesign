360 Degree Interactive Digital Artwork

For my MA I created a 360° video to create a virtual reality experience. I explored theories of life after death, wanting to confront mainstream contemporary beliefs which are held on the topic of death. My research covered the Hermetica and many more Ancient readings while also exploring how death is viewed throughout contemporary culture. The experience which was created represents a journey through light, from the visible light spectrum through to ultra violet light, this represents the journey from the physical dimension to the next.

The interactive 360° video may be viewed here.

The experience aims to challenge the audiences perspective, not only because the quotes which are in front of them can not be seen until the ultraviolet stage, but also the quotes employed to encourage questions on about existence and the limits of perception. The experience lasts beyond the artwork, as after the headset has been taken off the audience has conceptually entered the next realm, with a greater perspective and perception, not restricted by the bodily senses.
During the project the research was collected into a Visual Summary, which consists of a large volume of work, theories and background research into the themes which were explored within the project. The volumes naturally split into Primary and Secondary research, and are available upon request.
