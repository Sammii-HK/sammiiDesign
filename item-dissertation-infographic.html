Dissertation Communication Infographic

This piece was created as my dissertation for my BA Degree. It's an interactive Infographic which covers the History of Communication. I really wanted to push the boundaries of what was possible within designing my dissertation, not wanting to settle for an essay format.

The interaction of the infographic means that the design of the timeline can hold a wealth of information but not overload the audience as it only displays the specified topic at one time. This interaction also encourages engagement from the audience with the information that is being presented, as the user feels more in control.

I relished this opportunity to push my design skills and moulded the constraints of the brief so that I was able to create my first website from the information of my dissertation in a creative and unusual way, which actually enhanced the experience of engaging with the essay.

I am currently in the process of reworking this whole project and coding it for responsive web design.
