Final Major Project - BA

My Final Major Project visually communicates The Beauty Myth, by Naomi Wolfe, and themes which relate to the text. The book sits underneath an perspex cover, eteched onto it is a board room table, which is outlined by quotes from Aristotle and his peers, the chairs have been created with typography which uses FTSE 100 figures and their earnings.
I experimented with physical uses of paper within the book. The physical process of the interation of the paper tunnel represents the quote's meaning. On another spread I represented the quote by layering different weights of paper.
The photograph that is placed at the beginning of the book, it may seem counter-intuitive but it represents that those who benefit from the theories found within the book want this knowledge to remain hidden.
