Degree Show Invitations

As a companion piece to the Degree Show posters, I also created an invitation to the Degree Show.
The design on the back of the invitation is a list of many things which relate to graphic design, this is a mixture of designers, fonts, typography terms and a variety of mediums.
The act of unfolding the invitation relates not only to the quote but also speaks directly about the show.
